Specialization in PBX / IP-PBX Solutions

We specialize in the design, supply, installation, configuration, and support of PBX and IP-PBX communication systems, delivering reliable and scalable voice solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our expertise ensures seamless internal and external communication with enhanced call quality, security, and flexibility—whether on-premise or cloud-based.

We work with leading PBX and IP-PBX platforms to deliver customized communication solutions that align with your business requirements and budget.

Our PBX / IP-PBX Services Include:

  • IP-PBX System Design & Deployment
  • Traditional PBX to IP-PBX Migration
  • VoIP Integration & SIP Trunk Configuration
  • Call Routing, IVR & Auto Attendant Setup
  • Extension Configuration (Desk Phones & Softphones)
  • Voicemail, Call Recording & Call Analytics
  • Multi-Branch & Remote Office Connectivity
  • System Testing, Optimization & User Training

Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Our AMC services are designed to minimize downtime, improve system efficiency, and provide proactive support—allowing your business to operate smoothly without technical disruptions.
